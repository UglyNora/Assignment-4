The Art of MCL

MCL is a renowned artist who studied at what is formerly known as
The School Of Design at North Carolina State University.
She works in both oils and acrylics on canvas.
However, she prefers subtractive, stone sculpture,
despite the difficulties of procurring weighty, geologic materials.

Very little is publically known about the self proclaimed naïve artist except that she originally lived in North Carolina before relocating to the Miami metro area in 2003.

Currently, MCL resides in Valrico, FL. The majority of her present-day paintings depict close friends and family.
She uses Golden Acrylics on strectched canvas. It is important to note that these works are not for sale.
MCL is willing, on occassion, to barter.
